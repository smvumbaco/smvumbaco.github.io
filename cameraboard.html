Emerald Innovations: Camera Illumination PCB

One of the PCBs that I designed at Emerald was for an integrated camera module used with the Jetson TX2. This camera is used in dark environments, so I created a custom board that could connect to the camera module on both sides and illuminate the environment in IR light. This involved precise measurements of the board's outline and shape so it could connect to the existing PCB as well as an extreme consciousness towards heat dissipation and current draw, as the IR camera used drew a lot of curent. This PCB made studies using the camera module generate better data and show improved reliability going forward.
