Teledyne FLIR: 5mA Constant Current Board

At Teledyne FLIR, I worked in a Systems Engineering role where I mainly evaluated the integration of new hardware into robotic systems such as PackBot, MTRS, and SUGV. As a part of this effort, I completed the schematic, layout, assembly, and evaluation of 10 custom PCBAs that served as constant current sources used to evaluate the health of robots' electronics.

For this project, I was responsible for both component selection and board design. I completed both the schematic and layout designs in KiCad before generating the necessary gerber files to get the boards fabricated. Upon receiving the required parts, I soldered each component to the PCBAs and tested every board, resulting in 10 simple system evaluation tools that could be easily reproduced and repurposed for additional electronic applications.
