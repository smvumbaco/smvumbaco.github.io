Emerald Innovations: Jetson TX2 Carrier Board

A critical part of the system I was working on was the carrier board for the Jetson TX2 MCU. This large, complicated board with hundreds of components was a primary focus during my time at Emerald; I added subsystems and improved existing ones after rigorous testing and evaluation. I also updated the layout of this board, taking care to ensure that noise was mitigated and sensitive signals such as differential pairs were routed correctly and efficiently. After my work on this board, it was ordered to be used in all products going forward.
