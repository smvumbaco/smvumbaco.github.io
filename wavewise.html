Generate: WaveWise

WaveWise is an ocean farming venture seeking to simplify and commercialize ocean farming of kelp. The system consists of a surface buoy and a submersible, depth-adjustable buoy connected by a tethering system. The system uses sensor readings of water temperature, salinity, pH, UV levels, pressure, and nitrate levels to determine whether to raise or lower the farming platform to optimize plant growth. The design will incorporate custom PCBs and cables as well as extensive embedded development.

The surface buoy is responsible for power generation and will have a system of power switching between solar panels and a backup rechargeable battery. This buoy will communicate with the submersible buoy via a specialized cabling system. The submersible, in turn, carries and interprets the data of several different sensors. It also will contain an air compressur that forces water in and out of the chassis to adjust the depth of the buoy as needed.

WaveWise is curretly under production, so there are not many materials to display at this time. However, I am currently overseeing a group of 5 Electrical and Computer Engineers, as I am serving as my team's ECE Technical Lead. I am responsible for developing the system as well as delegating work and serving as the final say in design and purchase decisions.
