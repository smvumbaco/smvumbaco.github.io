Generate: AutoPasser

AutoPasser is an automatic hockey puck shooter. It is designed to fire street hockey pucks at a user-specified speed at regular intervals. This prototype saw 2 major development cycles. The first cycle was mainly a proof-of-concept that sought to ensure the firing and automatic loading subsystems were viable. The second cycle sought to massively overhaul this original design, adding rotation, remote control, and puck sensing features while decreasing both the footprint and weight of the device.

AutoPasser Version 1

The first prototype is showcased in the video linked above. The success of this prototype warranted a second revision that greatly expanded on the design and capabilities of the original design.
