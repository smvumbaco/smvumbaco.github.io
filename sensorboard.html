Emerald Innovations: Sensor PCB

The sensor PCB I worked on at Emerald included a wide variety of sensors. These sensors measured temperature, humidity, light, volume, and RF signals, among other things. For this board, I enhanced each subsystem both through hardware and software, improving filtering and writing to each device from the Jetson TX2 through the I2C protocol. Overall, I enhanced the capabilities of this PCB and made its functionality much more cost-effective.
